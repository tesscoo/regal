Our Team

Gary Klein, ChFC
President

First securities licensed in 1981, Gary is co-founder and president of Regal Wealth Group. Gary was born and raised in Southern California. He earned his B.A. in Philosophy at California State University, Fullerton and completed three years of graduate study at Fuller Theological Seminary. He also earned the Chartered Financial Consultant (ChFC) designation from American College in Pennsylvania. Gary is a member of the Financial Planning Association, the Society of Financial Services Professionals and the Investment Management Consultants Association. He is married to Melinda Davidson and has three children.

Bill Beeler
Senior Vice President

First securities licensed in 1991, Bill is co-founder and chief compliance officer of Regal Wealth Group. Bill was born and raised in Omaha, Nebraska. He received his bachelor's degree in engineering from Idaho State University and then his master's degree in management from USC. He served for eight years as a pilot in the U.S. Air Force and then flew for a major airline for several years. He and his wife, Jackie, have two children.

Christine Klein
Financial Advisor

Christine Klein joined Regal Wealth Group in 2005 as a client relations specialist before becoming a licensed investment advisor. She is currently enrolled at the University of California, Irvine (UCI) where she is in her second year in residence working towards her Certified Financial Planner, CFP designation. Before joining Regal, Christine obtained her bachelor's degree in psychology from UCI and worked as an applied behavioral therapist at the UCI Child Development Center for children with ADHD and other learning disorders. She is newly married and lives in Irvine.

Jodi Edwards
Client Relations Manager

Jodi Edwards joined Regal Wealth Group as client relations manager in December 2007. Before joining Regal, Jodi worked as administrative supervisor for a healthcare computer software company. She is currently pursuing her bachelor's degree in business management. Jodi is also a member of the Long Beach Emblem Club, a charitable organization that dedicates time and money to worthwhile projects, schools, and organizations within the community. Jodi lives in Seal Beach with her daughter, Ashley.
